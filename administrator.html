Administrators
Founder
Shri L.Adaikalaraj
Founder

Our Founder was a strategic leader late Mr. L. Adaikalaraj He was the member of parliament 4 consecutive periods. It was in his mind to provide rural youth a kind of education that will improve their quality of life.
His dream came true when he established Jenneys Academy of Hotel Management in the year 1996. Our founder knew that the Tourism sector is a sector which provides employment in a very large scale and he implemented innovative educational programs pertaining to hotel industry and initiatives, resulting in increased student enrolment and improved academic outcomes.
He developed strategic partnerships with domestic hotels and restaurants, fostering collaboration and providing students with valuable internship and job placement opportunities.
Nearly 3 decades have passed by and until now more than 3000 students have successfully passed out from our institution and our students are globally accepted and recognised.

I wish and pray that our students should rise up to be the best versions of themselves and realise all their dreams.

Principal
Mr.Paal Vanan
Principal

It gives me immense pride and gratitude as the principal of this esteemed institution. Our institution is more than just a building with classrooms and facilities. It is a place where dreams take flight, where knowledge is nurtured, and where characters are shaped. It is a hub of learning, innovation, and growth.
At the heart of our institution, we have a dedicated team of faculties and staff who strive to provide the highest quality education to the students. They are not merely educators, but mentors and guides who inspire, challenge, and encourage our students to reach their full potential.

We believe in the power of education to transform lives. Our goal is to equip our students with the skills, knowledge, and values necessary to thrive in an ever-changing world. We strive to create a learning environment that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and a love for lifelong learning.
In our institution, we value diversity and inclusivity. We celebrate the unique talents and perspectives of each individual, creating a rich tapestry of cultures and ideas. We believe that by embracing diversity, we prepare our students to become global citizens who can navigate and contribute to a multicultural society.
We also recognize the importance of holistic development. Alongside academic excellence, we emphasize the development of character, resilience, and ethical values. We encourage our students to engage in extracurricular activities, sports, and community service to cultivate well-rounded individuals who can make a positive impact on the world.
Collaboration is a cornerstone of our institution. We believe that by fostering a spirit of teamwork and cooperation, we create an environment where everyone can thrive. We encourage open communication, mutual respect, and the exchange of ideas among students, parents, and staff.
Lastly, I want to express my gratitude to the parents and guardians who have entrusted us with the education and well-being of their children. Your support and partnership are invaluable in creating a nurturing and successful learning environment.
As we move forward, let us continue to work together, motivated by a shared vision of excellence and a commitment to the growth and success of our students. Together, we can continue to build a legacy of educational excellence that will impact generations to come .
